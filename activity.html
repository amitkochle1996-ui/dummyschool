School Activities

Engaging Minds, Enriching Lives

Cultural Celebrations & Performances

At Dummy School, we believe in celebrating the rich tapestry of Indian culture and beyond. Our students actively participate in and organize various cultural events throughout the year, including annual day celebrations, traditional dance performances, classical music recitals, and folk art displays. These activities not only showcase their talents but also instill a deep appreciation for diverse cultural heritage.

We encourage participation in inter-school cultural competitions, providing a platform for students to hone their skills and represent the school with pride. Our cultural clubs, such as the Drama Club, Music Ensemble, and Dance Troupe, offer specialized training and opportunities for creative expression.

Sports & Athletics

Physical fitness and sportsmanship are integral to our educational philosophy. Dummy School offers a wide array of sports and athletic programs, catering to various interests and skill levels. Our facilities include a large playground, basketball courts, a cricket pitch, and indoor sports areas.

Students can participate in popular sports like Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, and Athletics. Regular coaching sessions are conducted by experienced trainers, and students are encouraged to join school teams and compete in district, state, and national level tournaments, fostering teamwork, discipline, and a healthy competitive spirit.

Academic Clubs & Competitions

Beyond the regular curriculum, Dummy School offers a variety of academic clubs designed to deepen students' understanding and passion for different subjects. These include the Science Club, Mathematics Club, Literary Society, Debate Club, and Robotics Club.

Students regularly participate in inter-school quizzes, Olympiads, science fairs, and debate competitions, which provide excellent opportunities to apply their knowledge, enhance problem-solving skills, and engage in healthy intellectual rivalry. These platforms encourage research, innovation, and independent thinking.

Community Service & Social Initiatives

Instilling a sense of social responsibility and empathy is a core value at Dummy School. Our students are actively involved in various community service projects and social initiatives. These include cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns on environmental issues, visits to old age homes and orphanages, and fundraising for charitable causes.

Through these activities, students learn the importance of giving back to society, developing leadership qualities, and understanding real-world challenges. Such experiences foster compassion, civic responsibility, and a commitment to making a positive difference in their communities.

Arts & Crafts Workshops

Creativity knows no bounds at Dummy School. We offer a wide range of arts and crafts workshops to encourage students to explore their artistic talents. These include painting, sketching, pottery, sculpture, origami, and various traditional Indian art forms like Warli painting and Madhubani art.

Our dedicated art studios and experienced instructors provide a nurturing environment for artistic expression. Students' artworks are regularly showcased in school exhibitions, fostering confidence and encouraging their unique creative journeys.

Student Exchange Program

Expand your horizons and experience new cultures firsthand with our enriching Student Exchange Program. We partner with schools in different countries to offer students a chance to live abroad for a semester, attend classes, and immerse themselves in a new environment. This program fosters global citizenship, independence, and a deeper understanding of the world.

Participants will have the opportunity to improve their language skills, build lifelong friendships with students from diverse backgrounds, and gain a unique perspective that will benefit them academically and personally. The experience is meticulously planned to ensure safety and a valuable learning journey.
