Welcome to Our Admissions

We are delighted that you are considering Dummy School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, supportive, and informative, ensuring that prospective families gain a comprehensive understanding of our values, academic programs, and vibrant school community. We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute positively to our diverse environment.

At Dummy School, we believe in creating an inclusive atmosphere where every student can thrive. Our dedicated admissions team is here to guide you through each step, answer your questions, and help you discover how Dummy School can be the perfect fit for your child's educational journey.

Our Admission Process

1. Inquiry & Prospectus

Begin by exploring our school prospectus and website. You can submit an online inquiry form to receive more information.

2. Application Form

Complete and submit the official application form along with all required documents and the application fee.

3. Entrance Assessment

Prospective students may undergo an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their academic readiness.

4. Interview

A personal interview with the student and parents/guardians will be scheduled with the admissions committee or principal.

5. Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an offer of admission.

6. Enrollment

Complete the enrollment formalities by submitting necessary fees and final documents to secure the admission.

Required Documents

For Student

  • Birth Certificate (Original for verification, copy for submission)
  • Previous Academic Records/Report Cards (Last 2-3 years)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs (recent)
  • Aadhar Card copy (if applicable)

For Parents/Guardians

  • Aadhar Card copies
  • PAN Card copies
  • Proof of Residence (e.g., utility bill, passport)
  • Passport-sized photographs

Please ensure all documents are complete and accurate to facilitate a smooth admission process. Original documents will be required for verification purposes.

Important Dates

Application Period

Applications for the academic year 2026-2027 open on October 15, 2025 and close on January 31, 2026.

Assessment & Interview Dates

Assessments and interviews will be conducted from February 15, 2026 to March 15, 2026. Specific slots will be communicated upon application submission.

Admission Offer & Enrollment

Admission offers will be extended by March 31, 2026. Enrollment and fee payment deadline is April 15, 2026.

We encourage early applications as seats are limited. Please refer to the school calendar for any updates or changes to these dates.
