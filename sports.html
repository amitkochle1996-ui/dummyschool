Sports & Athletics

Building Champions, Fostering Teamwork

Our Sports Philosophy

At Dummy School, sports and physical education are integral to our holistic development approach. We believe that participation in sports not only promotes physical fitness but also instills crucial life skills such as teamwork, leadership, discipline, resilience, and sportsmanship. Our aim is to provide every student with the opportunity to discover their athletic potential, pursue their passions, and develop a lifelong love for physical activity.

We offer a comprehensive sports program designed to cater to students of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to aspiring athletes. Our experienced coaches and state-of-the-art facilities ensure a safe and encouraging environment for all.

Outdoor Sports

Football

Our expansive football field provides the perfect setting for students to develop their skills in the world's most popular sport. Regular coaching sessions focus on technique, strategy, and teamwork, preparing our school teams for inter-school and district-level competitions.

Cricket

Cricket is a passion at Dummy School. With dedicated nets and a well-maintained pitch, students receive expert coaching in batting, bowling, and fielding. Our cricket teams consistently perform well in local and regional tournaments.

Basketball

Our outdoor basketball courts are always buzzing with energy. Students learn fundamental skills, participate in friendly matches, and compete in school and inter-school leagues.

Athletics

From sprints to long jump, our athletics program encourages individual excellence and perseverance. Students train for various track and field events, participating in annual sports meets.

Indoor Sports

Badminton

Our indoor badminton courts provide a dynamic space for students to develop agility, precision, and strategic thinking. Coaching is available for all levels, fostering both recreational play and competitive excellence.

Table Tennis

Table Tennis is a popular choice for developing quick reflexes and hand-eye coordination. Our dedicated tables and coaching ensure students can enjoy the sport and participate in tournaments.

Chess

The Chess Club hones strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. Students engage in friendly matches and prepare for inter-school chess championships.

Carrom

A traditional Indian indoor game, Carrom helps develop precision and focus. Students enjoy playing and competing in school-level tournaments.

Sports Events & Facilities

Dummy School hosts an annual Sports Day, inter-house competitions, and various sports tournaments throughout the year, celebrating athletic achievements and fostering a spirit of healthy competition. Our commitment to sports is reflected in our excellent facilities.

  • Spacious Football and Cricket Grounds
  • Multiple Basketball and Volleyball Courts
  • Indoor Badminton and Table Tennis Facilities
  • Dedicated Chess and Carrom Areas
  • Well-equipped Gymnasium (for older students)

We believe that through sports, our students learn invaluable lessons that extend far beyond the playing field, preparing them for success in all aspects of life.
