Dummy School

Where Learning Begins and Futures Are Built

The Dummy School is a co-educational School in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.

At Dummy School, we believe that education is more than just academics — it’s about nurturing young minds, building character, and inspiring lifelong curiosity.

Our school is a vibrant and inclusive community where students are encouraged to explore their potential, embrace creativity, and grow with confidence. With dedicated educators, innovative learning environments, and a commitment to excellence, Dummy School is a place where every child feels valued, supported, and ready to achieve greatness.
