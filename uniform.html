Official School Uniforms

Pre-Primary (Nursery - KG2)

Boys

  • Light Blue half-sleeve shirt.
  • Navy blue shorts.
  • White socks and black velcro shoes.

Girls

  • Light Blue tunic frock.
  • White socks and black velcro shoes.
  • Green hair accessories.

Primary & Middle School (Grades 1-8)

Boys

  • Light Grey half-sleeve shirt.
  • Charcoal trousers.
  • School tie, belt, and ID card.
  • Black leather shoes with laces.

Girls

  • Light Grey half-sleeve blouse.
  • Charcoal pleated skirt (knee-length).
  • School tie, belt, and ID card.
  • Black leather shoes and brown hair accessories.

Senior School (Grades 9-12)

Boys

  • Crisp White half-sleeve shirt with school monogram.
  • Black trousers.
  • School tie, belt, and ID card.
  • Black leather shoes with laces.

Girls

  • Cream Kurta with white piping.
  • Maroon Salwar.
  • White Dupatta with school monogram.
  • Black leather shoes.

All Grades (Winter Additions)

Boys (Grades 1-12)

  • Full-sleeve version of the regular shirt.
  • Navy blue V-neck pullover sweater.
  • Navy blue blazer with school crest (mandatory for Grades 6-12).
  • Regular trousers and shoes.

Girls (Grades 1-12)

  • Full-sleeve version of the regular blouse/Kurta.
  • Navy blue V-neck pullover sweater or cardigan.
  • Navy blue blazer with school crest (mandatory for Grades 6-12).
  • Regular skirt/Salwar and shoes. Grey woolen stockings for skirts.

Sports Uniform (All Grades)
